#  Social Media Checklist for College Students

##  Do's

- Think before posting anything online  
- Use strong privacy settings on your accounts  
- Share accurate and verified information  
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages  
- Build a positive and professional online presence  



##  Don'ts

- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not spread fake news or rumors  
- Avoid cyberbullying or trolling others  
- Do not waste excessive time on social media  



##  Conclusion

Responsible social media use helps maintain a positive digital footprint and ensures safety, professionalism, and respectful online interactions.
