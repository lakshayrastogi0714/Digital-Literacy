# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts and update them regularly.  
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, banking, and social media accounts.  
3. Do not click on suspicious links received through email, SMS, or social media. Always verify the source.  
4. Never share personal information such as passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone online.  
5. Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.  
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email.  
7. Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.  

## UPI & Financial Safety Tips
8. Never approve unknown UPI collect requests or payment requests from strangers.  
9. Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money through UPI.  
10. Do not scan random QR codes, especially those received from unknown sources.  
11. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930  

Stay alert and think before you click or share any information online.
